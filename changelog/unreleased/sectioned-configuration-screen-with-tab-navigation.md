The configuration screen now organizes settings into three
sections—Banner, General, and Widgets—instead of a single flat list.
Use `Tab` and `Shift+Tab` to move between sections, while `↑`/`↓`
still navigate items within and across sections. Setting descriptions
are clearer and written from the user's perspective.
