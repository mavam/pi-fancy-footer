The context bar widget now offers eight visual styles — blocks `■□▨`,
lines `━─┄`, circles `●○◎`, parallelograms `▰▱▰`, diamonds `◆◇❖`,
bars `█░▒`, stars `★☆★`, and specks `•◦•`. A new _context bar style_
setting in the configuration screen lets you pick the one you prefer.

Used cells are now colored with the widget's icon color so they stand out
from free and reserved cells, which stay dim.
